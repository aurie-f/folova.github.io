Locked Within

A decision based RPG game I started developing according to combinated knowledge from game design and blender courses. With vision in mind, I created concept of the game, 3D models in Blender and worked with UE blueprints to shape the gameplay further through mechanics and systems.

About The Game

    Your goal as the player is to escape the dungeon and learn why you were locked up.
    You meet various NPCs that give you tasks and through them you'll uncover the truth about the world and yourself.
    There are people trying to help you as well as those, who want you dead.
    Who will you trust?
